Persimmons

Enjoy some juicy, freshly pluck persimmons straight from your garden.

Description

Persimmons are my favourite fruits. October is generally considered the pumpkin season. But to me, they're persimmon season. I wait all year until october to finally buy this heavenly fruit. It is mouth-watering and wallet-emptying. But how do we devour it while being financially responsible? Hint: (My recipe)

Ingredients

  • Persimmon seeds
  • Soil
  • Water (a lot)
  • A dog (or) spade

Directions

  1. Go to your garden.
  2. Get your dog/spade to dig a hole in your desired area.
  3. Carefully place your seed in the hole.
  4. Use the soil you dug out to close up the hole.
  5. Pat it gently. (not too tight)
  6. Pat your dog and say, "Good boy/girl". (mandatory if you used your dog)
  7. Pour some water on top of it.
  8. Water it everyday in the morning.
  9. Wait for several years until it grows big and starts fruiting.
