Toast

Bite onto your crispy toast by making this amazing recipe.

Description

Crispy toast is easy to make. It is the perfect snack that you could make in 2 minutes. This recipe has been passed down in our family through generations that enjoyed the simplicity of this delicious recipe. It also spikes your glucose instantly, making it the perfect snack for your hypoglycemic episode.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Take the butter and apply it to your white bread generously on both sides.
  2. Heat up your favourite pan on your stove.
  3. Place the buttered bread carefully on the pan.
  4. Wait for 1 minute and flip it.
  5. Keep flipping until you are happy with the colour.
